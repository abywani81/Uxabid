The challenge

Healthcare access remains fragmented — patients juggle multiple apps for appointments, teleconsultations, medicine delivery, lab tests, and health records. Tabeebi aimed to consolidate these services into a single, intuitive mobile platform. The challenge was designing a comprehensive health ecosystem that serves diverse patient needs while maintaining simplicity, trust, and accessibility for users across varying digital literacy levels.

Design approach

A human-centered design process guided by extensive user research with patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers. We conducted 35+ user interviews, analyzed competitor gaps, and mapped the complete patient journey — from symptom checking to treatment adherence. The design prioritizes progressive disclosure, contextual help, and accessibility features (large touch targets, voice search, high contrast modes).

Visual identity

Tabeebi's mobile-first design features a calming dark theme with deep teal and blue gradients, accented with healing teal and warm orange for CTAs. The dark background reduces eye strain for users who may be using the app during late hours or in low-light environments. Large, tappable cards and clear typography create a trustworthy, clinical-yet-warm atmosphere.

📌 Key learnings & future iterations

  • ▹ Accessibility matters: Voice navigation and screen reader optimization added in v2
  • ▹ Offline capabilities: Added offline access to prescriptions for low-network areas
  • ▹ Trust signals: Verified doctor badges improved conversion rates
  • ▹ Family accounts: Managing dependents now a priority for next release

🗺️ Roadmap: What's next

Phase 2 includes AI-powered symptom checker with differential diagnosis, integration with wearable devices (Apple Health, Fitbit), telemedicine group consultations, and a mental wellness module. Expansion to 5 new cities planned for Q3 2026.

Tabeebi Patient app successfully consolidates fragmented healthcare services into a single, intuitive mobile platform. By prioritizing user research, thorough documentation, and accessible interaction design, we've created a solution that truly serves patients throughout their entire healthcare journey — from first symptom to complete recovery.
