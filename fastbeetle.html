Strategic Discoveries

Understanding Real Drivers

Behind user behavior and business constraints

🔍

Access Problem, Not Just Usability

The core challenge isn’t interface design — it’s creating infrastructure where none exists.

→ Solution: UX must focus on reducing operational friction, not just digital polish.

📈

Growth-First, Efficiency-Later Model

+187.75% YoY revenue growth, but ~96% profit drop signals aggressive expansion.

→ Solution: Product features must prioritize scale enablement over feature richness.

🤝

Trust is the Currency

In underserved markets, reliability and transparency matter more than speed.

→ Solution: Every touchpoint should reinforce predictability and accountability.

📌 Key Takeaways & Future Scope

  • ▹ Inclusive design for low-digital literacy drives real adoption in underserved regions
  • ▹ Offline capabilities built trust in erratic network zones across Tier-2/Tier-3 markets
  • ▹ Trust and transparency — not speed — are the true currency in emerging logistics markets
  • ▹ Next: AI-powered demand prediction + carbon-neutral delivery routes
