The challenge

Rural and semi-urban regions suffer from inefficient delivery networks — high costs, lack of real-time visibility, and unreliable service. Traditional logistic providers ignore these "last-mile dark zones". Fastbeetle aimed to bridge that gap with a lightweight, intuitive platform for both merchants and delivery partners.

Design approach

We adopted a mobile-first, accessibility-driven methodology. Through field research across 6 towns, we identified key friction points: package handover confusion, offline-first needs, and complex address systems. The result — a super-simple interface with voice-assisted address capture, smart route clustering, and low-data mode.

Visual identity

The interface uses deep charcoal gradients, amber accents (#F5B042), and bold typography — ensuring high contrast and readability in outdoor conditions. Every card sits on a subtle glass layer that complements the brand's reliability while feeling modern.

Strategy, impact & learnings

📋 User research synthesis

Over 120 interviews with small e‑commerce sellers and delivery agents. Key pain points: unreliable pickup windows, paper-based tracking.

⚙️ Product metrics

42% reduction in failed deliveries, 28% faster cycle, 94% customer satisfaction among small merchants.

🎯 Business integration

Powers shipping for 350+ regional D2C brands. Plug‑and‑play API connects with Shopify, WooCommerce.
