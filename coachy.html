Project Brief

Understanding the challenge

The corporate coaching program management enhancement is designed to streamline and optimize the management of corporate coaching programs. This feature aims to address the complexities involved in managing multiple participants, coaches, and administrative tasks within corporate coaching environments. By providing a more intuitive and efficient system, this enhancement will support better Edge and Driving Increased Revenue.

Risks

Potential risks & mitigation

Potential risks include the complexity of implementing a new system that integrates seamlessly with existing processes and data. There is also a risk of resistance to change from users accustomed to the current system. Ensuring robust training and support will be essential to mitigate these risks.

Goal of the Study

Research objectives

This study was initiated to explore the complexities and requirements of Managing Corporate Coaching Programs Effectively. The Primary Unknowns addressed include the Optimal Management Structure For Large-Scale Corporate Programs, the Integration of Financial And Administrative Roles; and the Customization of Coaching Experiences To Meet Diverse Corporate Needs. This Research Aims To Inform Decision On How To Streamline The Management Of Corporate Coaching Programs, Enhance Participant Engagement, And Ensure Alignment With Organizational Objectives.

Design Approach

User-centered design methodology

We applied a user-centered design methodology rooted in extensive stakeholder interviews. Over 8 weeks, we conducted 22 user interviews with HR leaders, certified coaches, and program participants across 5 organizations. Key insights revealed that administrators needed bulk operations and reporting, coaches wanted session notes and goal tracking, and participants craved visibility into their progress. The design solution centers on three tailored dashboards, role-based workflows, and a unified data model.

👔 Admin Flow

Import participants → Assign coaches → Track budgets → Generate reports

🎓 Coach Flow

View assigned participants → Log session notes → Update goal progress → Schedule follow-ups

💼 Participant Flow

View personal goals → Access session materials → Track progress → Submit feedback

📌 Challenges & key learnings

  • ▹ Role complexity: Designing for three distinct personas required extensive user journey mapping and iterative validation
  • ▹ Data privacy: Implemented granular permission layers at both API and UI level to ensure compliance
  • ▹ Payment flexibility: Built a dynamic pricing engine supporting per-session, retainer, and outcome-based billing models
  • ▹ Adoption strategy: Interactive onboarding flows reduced support tickets by 45% within the first quarter

🗺️ Future roadmap

Phase 2 includes AI-powered coach-participant matching based on personality and skill gaps, integration with Zoom/Teams for automatic session recording, and advanced analytics dashboards with predictive completion modeling. Mobile apps for iOS and Android planned for Q2 2026.

Coachy successfully addresses the operational pain points of corporate coaching programs through thoughtful UX design, extensive user research, and robust feature development. By serving three distinct user personas with tailored interfaces while maintaining a unified data model, we delivered a solution that reduces administrative overhead, improves goal tracking visibility, and streamlines payment reconciliation — ultimately driving increased revenue and better coaching outcomes.
