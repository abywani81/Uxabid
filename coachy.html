The challenge

Corporate coaching programs face significant administrative overhead — managing diverse participant needs across departments, tracking individual progress toward goals, generating compliance reports, and handling complex payment structures. Existing solutions were either overly simplistic (spreadsheets) or enterprise-bloated (expensive, hard to use). The challenge was to build a unified web app that serves three user personas: HR administrators, coaches, and participants — each with distinct workflows and priorities.

Design approach

We applied a user-centered design methodology rooted in extensive stakeholder interviews. Over 8 weeks, we conducted 22 user interviews with HR leaders, certified coaches, and program participants across 5 organizations. Key insights revealed that administrators needed bulk operations and reporting, coaches wanted session notes and goal tracking, and participants craved visibility into their progress. The design solution centers on three tailored dashboards, role-based workflows, and a unified data model.

Visual identity

Coachy's interface features a sophisticated dark theme with deep purple-black gradients, vibrant mint and coral accents for interactive elements, and clean sans-serif typography. The design prioritizes clarity and reduces eye strain during long administrative sessions. Card-based layouts, subtle shadows, and consistent spacing create a professional yet approachable atmosphere that builds trust for enterprise users.

📌 Challenges & key learnings

  • ▹ Role complexity: Designing for three personas required extensive user journey mapping
  • ▹ Data privacy: Implemented granular permission layers at API and UI level
  • ▹ Payment flexibility: Built dynamic pricing engine supporting multiple billing models
  • ▹ Adoption strategy: Interactive onboarding reduced support tickets by 45%

🗺️ Future roadmap

Phase 2 includes AI-powered coach-participant matching based on personality and skill gaps, integration with Zoom/Teams for automatic session recording, and advanced analytics dashboards with predictive completion modeling. Mobile apps for iOS and Android planned for Q2 2026.

Coachy successfully addresses the operational pain points of corporate coaching programs through thoughtful UX design, extensive user research, and robust feature development. By serving three distinct user personas with tailored interfaces while maintaining a unified data model, we delivered a solution that reduces administrative overhead, improves goal tracking visibility, and streamlines payment reconciliation.
