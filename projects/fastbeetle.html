FastBeetle — Delivery UX Redesign

Improving trust, reducing drop-offs, simplifying delivery experience

← Back

Overview

Redesigning a hyperlocal delivery platform to improve trust and usability.

Problem

Users faced unclear delivery times and a complex checkout flow.

Objective

Increase transparency and reduce friction in ordering.

Research

Conducted interviews, competitor analysis, and user journey mapping.

Insights

Users value predictability over speed.

Solution

Introduced real-time ETA, simplified checkout, and live tracking UI.

Impact

+40% retention, -30% drop-offs.
